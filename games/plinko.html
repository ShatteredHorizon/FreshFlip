Plinko - Drop the ball

Watch the ball bounce and land on a multiplier

How to Play

  • Select your risk level (Low, Medium, or High)
  • Choose the number of rows
  • Click "DROP BALL" to release the ball
  • The ball will bounce off pegs and land in a slot
  • Win the multiplier shown in your slot!
