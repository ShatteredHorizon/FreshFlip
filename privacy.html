Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your data

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

At FreshFlip, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using FreshFlip, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, secure gaming environment. This policy outlines our practices in compliance with applicable data protection laws including GDPR and similar regulations.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email, Discord ID when using OAuth
  • Verification Data: Identity documents for KYC compliance
  • Financial Data: Wallet addresses, transaction history
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Data: Game history, bets placed, deposits, withdrawals
  • Communication Data: Support tickets, chat messages

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your account
  • Service Provision: Running games, processing bets
  • Security: Fraud prevention, account verification
  • Compliance: Legal obligations, KYC/AML requirements
  • Communication: Account updates, promotions (with consent)
  • Analytics: Improving our services

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, payment processing, identity verification
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or for legal proceedings
  • Affiliate Partners: With your consent for promotions

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your game history or financial data for marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS for all data transmission
  • Storage Encryption: Sensitive data encrypted at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to necessary data
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring
  • 2FA: Two-factor authentication available
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments performed quarterly

While we use industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but actively work to protect your data.

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information.

9. Children's Privacy

FreshFlip is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover account information from a minor, we will close the account and destroy the data.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored on servers outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place including standard contractual clauses and adequate protection measures.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or platform notification. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@freshflip.com or through our support system.
