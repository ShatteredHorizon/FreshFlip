Getting Started

Welcome to FreshFlip! Here's how to begin your gaming journey:

  • Create an account using Discord OAuth or email/password
  • Complete your profile with a username and avatar
  • Make your first deposit using supported cryptocurrencies
  • Choose from 12 exciting games to play
  • Claim daily rewards and refer friends for bonuses

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deposit funds?
Navigate to your wallet section and click deposit. We support BTC, ETH, USDT, and other major cryptocurrencies. Copy the deposit address and send funds from your wallet.
What is the minimum bet amount?
Minimum bets vary by game. For most games, the minimum is $1 or equivalent in your currency. Some games allow bets as low as $0.10.
How do I withdraw winnings?
Go to Wallet > Withdraw, enter your wallet address and amount, then confirm. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.
Is FreshFlip provably fair?
Yes! All games use cryptographic seeds that you can verify. Check our Provably Fair page for verification tools.
How does the referral program work?
Share your referral code with friends. When they sign up and play, you earn 30% of their house edge. They also get a starting bonus!
What happens if I disconnect during a game?
Your bet is secured. If disconnected, the game auto-completes and winnings are credited to your balance automatically.
How do I contact support?
Use the contact form below or join our Discord server for real-time help.

Game Guides

Mines - Find gems, avoid bombs
Select how many mines (1-5). Click tiles to reveal. Each gem multiplies your bet. Cash out anytime before hitting a mine!
Crash - Cash out before crash
Watch the multiplier rise. Cash out before it crashes! Later cash out = bigger multiplier but more risk.
HiLo - Higher or lower?
Guess if the next card is higher or lower than yours. Each correct guess increases your multiplier by 1.25x!
Dice - Roll over target
Set your target number (1-100). Roll the dice - if roll is below your target, you win with the selected multiplier!
Blackjack - Beat the dealer
Get cards closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Blackjack (21 on first two cards) pays 3:2!
Plinko - Ball drop
Drop balls and watch them bounce through pegs. Where they land determines your multiplier!
